    Flaunt the Bold with Red Dresses
    How many times have you spotted your favourite celebrities flaunting sexy red dresses on the red carpet? Some fashion pundits are even of the opinion that red has become the new black! Yes, the colour red is back with a vengeance, and its frenzy has caught up with women of all ages! From short dresses to long stylish gowns in differing shades of red, the colour seems to be soaring in popularity! At Myntra, you will find the most comprehensive selection of red dresses to choose from – shop now!

    Red Maxi Dress

    If you are a little uncomfortable wearing a mini dress, try a red maxi dress to add some flair to your wardrobe! Not only does it accentuate your body curves, but also offers a great deal of comfort. Flaunt a maxi dress at any social party or cocktail dinner to unleash that extra zing of glamour with a complementing glitzy clutch. Alternatively, wear some statement jewellery like an eye-catchy neckpiece or a chunky bracelet for a complete look!

    Red Lace Dress

    The red lace dress has been the rage ever since A-listers like Eva Longoria and the teen sensation Selena Gomez were seen flaunting them. You can browse through a wide selection of red lace dresses from D Muse or Vovoka to find the perfect hue of red you're looking for.

    Red Peplum Dress

    A red peplum dress is among the obvious choices for those fashion conscious women who wish to highlight their waistline. At Myntra, these chic and vintage-styled dresses are available in colour-blocked shades of red and black, a lethal combination! Whether it is an evening soiree or a formal event, you can play around with this versatile red party dress and accessorise it to make a unique style statement.

    Red Bodycon Dress

    If you are body confident, a red bodycon dress is the perfect way of demonstrating your self-esteem. Since this dress clings to your body, accessorize with a sleek belt, an equally appealing pair of stilettos, a shrug, and a scarf.

    Red Polka Dotted Dress

    A trendy option of red dress for girls would be the cute and playful red polka dotted dress, hands down! DressBerry and The Vanca have some delightful options in this category. Whether you're spending an evening out with friends or are out on a romantic date, a polka dotted red dress would be ideal with bright red matching lips!

    Indian Dress in Red

    The beautiful Indian dresses in red are not far behind when it comes to popularity in terms of colours! Red is the colour of the season and is trending big time in the ethnic wear scenario as well. Myntra has a vibrant selection of red dresses ranging from the elegant red chiffon numbers to the sparkling red sequin variants. Buy red dresses, and go bold and daring with a chic monochromatic look. Accentuate it with the perfect pair of shoes and a clutch. Most importantly, do not refrain from adding that extra red on your lips along with smoky eyes to glam up for a party! With Myntra at your fingertips, you need not look elsewhere. Pick your very own red dress online from the various styles that are sure to suit every body shape.
